Design Principles

How I think about design.

The lenses I bring to every product, screen, and detail.

01

Visual Hierarchy

Guide attention through scale, contrast, spacing, and motion — the eye should know exactly where to go.

02

Consistency

One system, repeated everywhere. A shared token library means the design scales without breaking.

03

Usability

Intuitive. Accessible. Easy. Beauty without function is decoration — real design serves people.

04

Whitespace as Structure

Negative space isn't empty — it's the architecture of attention, breath, and emphasis.

05

Typography

Readable, expressive, intentional. The right typeface can carry personality without a single icon.

06

Product Thinking

Beauty + user goals + business goals. Good design lives at the intersection of all three.

Process

From idea to interface.

A four-phase loop I iterate through on every project.

D1

Discover

Audit the brand, the user, the problem space, and the constraints.

D2

Define

Lock goals, scope, user flows, information architecture, and success metrics.

D3

Design

Wireframes → visual design → hi-fi prototypes with components and motion.

D4

Deliver

Prototype, test, hand off, iterate — and keep refining post-launch.
